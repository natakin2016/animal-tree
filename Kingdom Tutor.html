ANIMALS TREE

Information about Classification


Mammals

  • give birth to their young and feed them with milk.
  • have hair on at least part of their body.
  • have four limbs with digits ending in claws, nails, or hooves (except whales).
  • breathe with lungs.
  • are warm-blooded.
