What is the difference between a German Shepard and a Gray Wolf?


The German Shepherd is classified as a canis lupus familiaris and a Gray Wolf is classified as a canis lupus.


How did they come to this classification?


Canis lupus, Canis lupus familiaris, homo sapiens are their scientific names based on criteria of classification. All organisms are classified by asking a series of yes/no based on their characteristics.


Why?

The world is a global community and the classification system gives us a common language. Homo sapiens is understood as the genus species for humans around the world.
