What is the difference between a German Shepard and a Gray Wolf?


The German Shepherd is classified as a canis lupus familiaris and a Gray Wolf is classified as a canis lupus.




How did they come to this classification?


Canis lupus, Canis lupus familiaris, homo sapiens are their scientific names based on criteria of classification. Animals are classified or sorted by their similar characteristics.

The essential questions are:
  • How are organisms alike or different?
  • How do organisms meet their needs for survival?
  • How can we use the characteristics we observe to group them?


Why do we need to classify animals?

The world is a global community and the classification system gives us a common language. Homo sapiens is understood as the genus species for humans around the world.
